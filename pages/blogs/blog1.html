Welcome to Level Up!

We're glad you found us, welcome to Level Up! As a stem-based learning program, our initiative is to imporove your children's cognitive learning skills and ways of thinking outside of the box. We put a heavy focus on math, reflexes, reading, puzzle solving, and adventure all while having fun! Will kids know that they're learning? Probably not, which is part of the beauty of a stem-based program! It's like dicing vegetables into tiny pieces and hiding them in their foods so that they still hit their nutritional goals to grow healthy and strong, all the while you're not having to fight them to eat it!


While your kids will have fun on a daily basis and beg you not to leave, we can't stress enough how organized are daily programs are. Each day has a set curriculum planned well in advance with ___ from the second that your child is picked up or dropped off. (And yes, we offer pickups from different schools within the area.)


While our iniative will always remain the same, the activities and lessons that your children will take part in are always kept up to date with modern strategies and "hip" age-level interests. To learn more about the current activities that your kids will be a part of on a weekly basis, check out each one by clicking here.


Along with most activities, we have events that take place on a regular basis. As another factor of keeping kids ingauged is by having a little friendly competition from time to time. Some events of which have a ranking system in place so that the top-finishing students earn prizes, such as gift cards, store credit to our toy store "The Item Shop" (learn more here), and pizza! You can view rankings and all events by viewing the events tab up top. Make sure to register your kids for each event that they're interested in taking part in.


Please be sure to view all the beautiful, and some horifying, faces of our awesome staff members here. Each member of our team is fully dedicated to pushing your kid to the next level. Our staff members play and compete with students, help with homework so that you dont have to stay up late at night pulling your hair out, and become close friends with each and every student. All of which creates an amazing environment that encapsulates your children into forgetting that they're learning and growing stronger mentally and socially.


At this point, we're really not sure what to say, other than to ask why the heck you haven't signup up your child(ren) yet. Registration for both our After School and Summer Camp programs are always open. And shoot, if you're not ready to pull the trigger, plan a visit to get a better feel of our program and environment. Either way, we can't wait to help your kid LEVEL UP!
