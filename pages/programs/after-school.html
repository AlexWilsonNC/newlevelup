Common Questions:

Q. How are "bused" schools transported to your facility?

• Bused students are transported via CMS Transportation to our facility. Parents of students who attend bused schools will need to submit an "Alternate Stops" Request to CMS Transportation prior to their student(s) attending LU.

Q. The school my student attends is not listed...

• If the school your student(s) attends is not listed, please contact us to see if transportation services can be made available.

Q. How do you decide if you can provide transportation services for schools you don't currently pick-up from?

• We operate on a time slot system, if the school your student attends dismisses during an open time slot, is within a reasonable distance of our facility, and does not conflict with our current Transportation Schedule, we may be able to provide transportation services.

Q. How do you decide which schools you will provide transportation services for?

• Our Transportation Schedule is based on a first-come, first-serve basis, and may also change throughout the year based on changes in enrollment.

Q. What can I do if you don't have space in your program and/or you don't provide transportation services for my student's school?

• Parents may register their student on our Enrollment Waitlist, which gives them priority for enrollment when Transportation Services and/or space in our program becomes available.

Q. Can I provide my own transportation to your facility for my student(s)?

• Parents are welcome to provide their own alternative form of transportation if desired.
