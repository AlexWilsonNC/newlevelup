Summer Camp

Our STEM based Summer Camp features 11 awesome weeks of LEGO based robotics and building, physical sports, eSports, Pokemon TCG, innovative arts, crafts, swimming, and much more; for campers between the ages of 4-14!

Registration:

$35 per Student

Rate:

$160 per Week

Sardis Forest Swim Club: (Required for pool)

Extended Care Info:

Since most campers will be exhausted by the conclusion of program hours, activities conducted during Extended Care hours are usually semi-structured and more relaxed to give students time to cool off, rest, and recover.

Camp Includes:

• Program hours from 9am-3pm, with extended care from 7am-6:30pm.

• We take arts and crafts to the geek level! With projects that inspire imagination, creativity, and allow campers to express and impress themselves.

• Visit places within the community for awesome activities like bowling, rollerskating, mini-golf, big jumping, movies, tabletop games, and wild life exhibitions.
