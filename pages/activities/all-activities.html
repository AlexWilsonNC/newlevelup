Level Up with the Pokemon TCG

Our competitive Pokémon Trading Card Game (PTCG) League and Tournament World Learn how to play like a pro while enhancing skills like math, reading, comprehension, syntax, critical thinking, strategy, problem solving, and more!

Battle against other participants in ranked league play, and compete in tournaments for awesome prizes! Get ready to be the very best, like no one ever was, in Battle Tower!

Advantages of Learning:


  • • Reading Comprehension
  • • Critical Thinking
  • • Math
  • • Planning
  • • Probability
  • • Sportmanship

Level Up with eSports

Our competitive Esports League and Tournament World Learn combos and timing, opponent based adaptation, crafting winning strategies, teamwork and cooperation (synergy!), and techniques and exercises to strengthen reaction time and hand-eye coordination. Battle with and against other participants in a variety of games in ranked league play, and compete in tournaments for awesome prizes!

Advantages of Learning:


  • • Critical Thinking
  • • Dexterity
  • • Sportmanship
  • • Reading
  • • Planning
  • • Probability

Level Up with Lego Building

Learn about the pieces, how to sort like a pro, and a variety of building techniques using real world engineering and architectural principles. Put your building skills to the test in the Chopping Block, our once per Season building competition that challenges participants to compete as a team to create a themed model using the techniques they've learned. Get ready to be crowned a Master Builder in Brickcrafting!

Advantages of Learning:


  • • Architecture
  • • Design
  • • Physics
  • • Problem Solving
  • • Puzzle Solving
  • • Teamwork

Level Up with Beyblades

Learn how parts and stadiums affect your spin, techniques that will help you rip your best, and the physics behind it all! Build your Bey from over a 100 different Beyblade Burst parts, and compete in league and tournaments to earn awesome prizes.

Advantages of Learning:


  • • Building
  • • Physics
  • • Competition
  • • Hand-Eye Coordination
  • • Probability
  • • Sportmanship
