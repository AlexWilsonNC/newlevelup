  • After School 21-22 [The Nexus] Program Terms and Conditions

     

    I am a Parent/Legal Guardian of the named Participant, and in consideration for my participant being permitted to attend the After School Program 2021-2022 [The Nexus] (hereinafter referred to as "The Nexus") conducted by Level Up Innovative STEMulation INC. (hereinafter referred to as “LU”) I, on behalf of my participant, myself, any personal representatives, heirs, and next of kin, hereby acknowledge and agree to the following:

     

    Billing

    1. Tuition Rate - I understand that the amount of my participant's tuition is based on the plan I choose for my participant, and the registration date.
    2. Tuition Due Date  - I understand that tuition is due monthly by the last day of the month preceding each month my participant is registered to attend The Nexus, until May of the following year; after which a prorated charge will made for any weeks and/or days registered in June of that year.
    3. Payment - I understand that tuition must be paid by the due date, and may be paid using a debit or credit card via the Procare Mobile and Desktop apps.
    4. Automatic Payments - I understand I have the option to authorize automatic payments by saving a valid debit or credit card to my participants Procare account, and activating Autopay.
    5. Cash and Check Payments - I understand that if I would like to pay using cash or check, it must be received by the the business day (business days are considered Monday-Friday) preceding the due date. Cash and check payments must be made in person Monday-Friday, from 11AM-6PM.
    6. Declined Payments - I understand a $15 Processing Fee will be billed to my participant's account any time a payment is declined.
    7. Payment Arrangement Agreements - I understand that if I would like to make any payments after the due date, a Payment Arrangement Agreement Form must be submitted at least 3 days before the tuition due date. Payment Arrangement Agreements are available to RETRO and CORE plans only.

     

    Non-Payment of Tuition and Other Fees

    1. I understand my registration reserves a spot for The Nexus from the first day to the last day of my child's school term, as long as payment for each upcoming registered month is made in advance or by the due date, or according to any approved Payment Arrangement Agreements.
    2. I understand that if:
      1. Payment is not received (or successfully processed) by the last day of the month(s) preceding the month(s) my Partcipant is registered to attend, and
      2. A Payment Arrangement Agreement has not been submitted and approved
    3. Then:
      1. The payment due will be billed to my participant's account as a balance.
      2. My participant's spot is no longer considered to be reserved, and is made available to LU's The Nexus waitlist and the general public, and
      3. My participant will not be allowed to attend The Nexus until payment is received, or a Payment Arrangement Agreement has been approved.
    4. I authorize LU to charge any card saved to my participant's account at any time to settle any unpaid balance. I understand that any unpaid balance that is not settled within 3 months of billing will have collection action taken; whether or not the participant associated with the account is currently attending, enrolled, or cancelled.

     

     

    Cancellation Policy

    1. I understand that LU assumes my intention is to register my participant for the entire school term, unless otherwise expressly communicated in writing (email)
    2. I understand I may cancel my child's The Nexus registration by submitting an express written request (via email) by at least 3 business days before the due date for the upcoming month my participant is registered to attend.

     

     

    Refund Policy

    I understand that all payments made to LU are non-refundable. I understand this also includes payments for participants who attend partial days/weeks, are absent, are asked to be picked up, suspended, or expelled from the program because of Behavioral and Disciplinary Policy violations.

    Please reference the Parent Handbook for Behavior and Discipline Policies.

     

    Late Pick-Up Policy

    1. Pick-up Time - I understand that my participant must be picked-up each day they attend The Nexus by 630PM.
    2. Late Pick-up Fee - I understand a late pick-up fee of five dollars ($5) will be billed to my participant's account for each fifteen (15) minute increment past the pick-up time that my participant is still in LU's care.
    3. Late Pick-Up Fee Waiver - I understand that LU may waive late pick-up fees under two (2) conditions:
      1. LU is notified by 645PM that my participant will be picked-up late, and
      2. LU is provided with an accurate pick-up time.
    4. Waiver Exceptions - I understand that if I communicate an inaccurate pick-up time, and I do not contact LU to update my estimated pick-up time within fifteen (15) minutes of the missed pick-up time then:
      1. I may not have late pick-up fees waived, and
      2. I may be charged all applicable late pick-up fees.

     

    Lost Items

    I understand that LU is not responsible for the recovery, storage, or return of items left in the participant storage areas (cubbies), vehicles, facility, grounds, and/or field trip locations. Participants and Parents/Legal Guardians are responsible for removing personal belongings from the LU facility and/or grounds each day. Items found in the participant storage areas (cubbies), vehicles, facility, and/or grounds, will be placed in the Lost & Found Container. Any items left in the Lost & Found Container at the end of The Nexus will be discarded or donated to a charity of LU's choice. Please be aware, some items may be considered trash, and be immediately discarded (items like food, paper products, etc.).

     

    Discipline Policy

    I have read and understand LU's Behavior and Discipline Policy (included in the Parent Handbook), and agree to accept any action deemed necessary by LU in accordance with that policy.

     

    Transportation Authorization

    I authorize LU to transport my participant as necessary for activities, field trips, and anything else deemed necessary by the Program Director.

     

    Photography Release

    I authorize LU to capture, stream, obtain, store, and/or use without payment, any photographs and/or videotapes of my participant for public relations, marketing, advertising, promotion, and/or internal training purposes; and releases from any claim or liability related to that use; waiving all claims for myself, my participants, child(ren)/ward(s), and any heirs, or next of kin.

     

    Release and Waiver of Liability and Indemnity Agreement

    1. I have inspected and carefully considered LU premises, facilities, and equipment; and accept them as being safe and suitable for my participant's use and participation in The Nexus.
    2. I acknowledge and agree that my participant's involvement in The Nexus activities may include risks, which could result in physical and/or emotional injury, loss, and/or property damage; and as a condition of my participant’s involvement, assume the aforementioned risk, and hereby agree to indemnify and save and hold harmless LU, its directors, officers, employees, and agents for any bodily injury, loss, liability, damage, or cost that I may incur due to my participant’s involvement in The Nexus.
    3. I acknowledge and agree that my participant’s involvement in The Nexus is voluntary, and I willingly elect my participant to attend despite the risks.
    4. I agree to immediately discontinue attendance if, at any time, I believe that The Nexus conditions are unsafe, or that my participant is unable to safely attend due to physical/mental conditions.
    5. I agree that in the event my participant requires immediate medical attention for injuries that occur while participating in The Nexus, and I am not present or able to communicate my desires at the time of injury, I authorize LU Team Members to give my participant reasonable first aid, and to arrange transport of my participant to a health care facility for emergency care as needed.
    6. I represent that I have adequate insurance to cover any injury or damage that my participant may suffer or cause while participating in The Nexus; or I agree to bear the costs of such injury or damage.
    7. I understand and agree to be responsible for the servicing, repair, or replacement of any property belonging to any party that is damaged or broken by my participant.
    8. I agree that this Release and Waiver of Liability and Indemnity Agreement is intended to be as broad and inclusive as is permitted by the law of the state of North Carolina, and that if any portion thereof is held invalid the remaining portions shall remain in full legal force and effect.
